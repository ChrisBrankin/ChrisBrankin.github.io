The Oral surgeon:

All dentists are trained in oral surgery but some choose to specialise in more complex work.
This involves removal of teeth that are positioned in such a way that it would not be possible to remove them in a normal dental practice or in patients with medical problems than can be more easily managed in a dental hospital.
