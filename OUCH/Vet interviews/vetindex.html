This video clip resource is for you if you’re considering studying veterinary science

Veterinary surgeons work to diagnose and treat sick animals across a range of different species.

They are also involved in disease surveillance and managing disease outbreaks to protect both animal and human health. 

It’s a profession full of variety and the opportunities are diverse. Vets are healthcare professionals for the animal kingdom.

One vet takes on roles carried out by many separate individuals in human medicine, such as surgery, radiology, prescribing and dispensing medicines and preventative healthcare – and on animal species from aardvarks to zebras.

The vets you will hear from work in a range of areas.  

These include small animal general practice, mixed practice and veterinary specialists.  

There are also some additional short videos that cover tips for getting work experience and how veterinary careers are structured.
