The GP:

Doctors who work in General Practice treat all common and long term medical conditions.

They refer patients to hospitals and other medical services for urgent and specialist treatment.

They focus on the health of the whole person combining physical, psychological and social aspects of care.

They will often have very long term realtionships with patients and their families.
