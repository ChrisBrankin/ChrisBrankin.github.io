The Physiotherapist:

Physiotherapists help to restore movement and function when someone is affected by injury, illness or disability.
They can also help to reduce the risk of injury or illness in the future.
They take a holistic approach that involves the patient in their own care.

After qualifying physiotherapists working in a number of specialties, they then choose to work predominantly in one area. This may be musculoskeletal, rehabilitation after stroke or a neurological problem, helping people with heart or respiratory disorders.
