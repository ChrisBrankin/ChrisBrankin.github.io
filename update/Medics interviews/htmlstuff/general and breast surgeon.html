The general and breast surgeon:

General surgeons can operate on a range of problems in an emergency.

Their emergency service is known as “on call”

Breast surgeons specialise in treating diseases of the breast in men and women. Some will be cancerous whilst others are harmless.

They work in teams with nurses, radiologists and radiographers to deliver a ‘one stop shop’ to their patients.
