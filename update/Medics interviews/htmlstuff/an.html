The Anaesthetist:

Anaesthetists are the largest group of hospital-based specialists.

They give anaesthetics for surgical, medical and psychiatric procedures.

They also help pregnant women achieve pain free child-birth, resuscitate acutely unwell patients in the emergency department and on wards, run chronic pain services and lead intensive care units.

They are a central part of the team needed to transfer acutely ill or severely injured patients between hospitals.

Some will also attend the site of major trauma to begin to stabilise the patient before they get to hospital.
