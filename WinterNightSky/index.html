The early evening Winter skies
February 2021

Some basic observing of The Moon, the Planets and The Constellations during the early evenings of the Winter months.

Clearly all three can be observed with the NAKED EYE but BINOCULARS will show craters on The Moon and The Galilean Moons of Jupiter ..
.. while observing The Moon through a SMALL TELESCOPE will knock your socks off as will viewing the rings of Saturn and the occasional double star.

Attached left are monthly skyguides containing observing lists, skymaps and photos.

The Skymaps were produced using Cartes du Ciel

The Observing lists were produced using Stellarium
