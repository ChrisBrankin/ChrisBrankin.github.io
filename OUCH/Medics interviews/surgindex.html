Surgery is the branch of medicine that deals with injuries, diseases and conditions that are treated with an operation or other procedure that opens up a body cavity or limb.

After their first few years as a general doctor, all surgeons begin the same initial surgical training. However later on, they specialise in a particular parts of the body.

That is because the operations can be very complex and a lot of research evidence shows that the best outcomes for patients are achieved when their operation is done by a surgeon who performs that operation very frequently.
