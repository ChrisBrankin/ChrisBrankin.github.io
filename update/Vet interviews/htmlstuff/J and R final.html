Small Animal General Practice:

These vets are the ones that most people think of when they think about veterinary practice.  

Many will have practices in the local high street.  

They look after companion animals, the majority of these are cats and dogs but can also include guinea pigs, rabbits and hamsters.  

Some will offer care for birds and more exotic pets such as lizards and spiders.  

They provide routine health care, disease screening and advice, manage chronic conditions and undertake the ‘routine’ surgeries, such as neutering, lump removals and taking x-rays.  

Some will have additional expertise in specific areas.  

They will refer to specialists for advice when needed and can refer animals onwards to referral units if they require more specialised care or procedures.
