The Orthodontist:

Orthodontists are dentists who specialise in treating people with misalignment of crooked, protruding or crowded teeth and to correct problems with the bite of the teeth.

These problems can mean it's harder to keep teeth and gums clean and the teeth are more likely to become damaged.

Orthodontics can also help in the treatment of other health problems, such as a cleft lip and palate.
