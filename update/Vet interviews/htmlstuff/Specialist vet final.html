Specialist Vet:

These vets have chosen to focus on specific areas of veterinary practice and have undertaken additional training in that field.  

They offer care for more complex and unusual conditions.  

They work in conjunction with general practitioners across a wide geographic area to offer advice and in some cases, direct care, to animals who require specialised interventions.
